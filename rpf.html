RED PLANET FARMING

NINA DEMIRJIAN

A science-based strategy game about growing enough food to feed a Martian settlement. Launched on Steam May 2020.

Red Planet Farming is a casual, single-player strategy game for PC made in Unity (C#). I created a prototype of the game in September 2018, and in 2019, the game won the NYU & Alfred P. Sloan Foundation Games Production Grant. The game has also been featured at MAGFest 2020.

Red Planet Farming is currently published on CoolMath Games, and launched on Steam May 25th, 2020.

Red Planet Farming currently has over 30,000 downloads and over 500 reviews, with a 96% approval rating on Steam.

I am the developer and designer of Red Planet Farming. My team also includes artist Sean Park and sound designer Sean Porio.
