ABUNDANCE

NINA DEMIRJIAN

A large-scale, interactive floor projection for the American Museum of Natural History's special exhibit, Unseen Oceans. Opened March 2018.

Abundance is a set of two large-scale floor projects. Each projection uses a Kinect to track the movement of visitors. Schools of fish flock and swim together in a circle, but will swim away from tracked visitors.

Abundance is a piece about conservation and overfishing in oceans. Too much "human activity" will cause the fish to quickly swim away and disappear from the screen.
